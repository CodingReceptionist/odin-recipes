Shepherds Pie

Description

Back to Ireland we go! Shepherds Pie is a classic! Maybe not a week night dinner but definitely a great one!

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 1/3 cups of mashed potatos

Steps

  1. Cook beef and onion in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until well browned, stirring to break up meat. Pour off fat.
  2. Stir black pepper and corn into skillet. Spoon beef mixture in 9-inch pie plate.
  3. Spoon potatoes over beef mixture.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned.
